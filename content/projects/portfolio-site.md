The site you're reading. A single-page portfolio on Laravel, Livewire, Vue, and
Tailwind, with content managed as version-controlled flat files. It deliberately
pairs two interactivity models: a server-driven Livewire contact form and a
client-driven Vue island for filtering these very projects. Fully tested (PHPUnit)
with CI running on every push.
